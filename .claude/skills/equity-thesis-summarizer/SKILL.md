---
name: equity-thesis-summarizer
description: Create structured stock writeups including bull case, bear case, catalysts, and risk factors.
---

## When to use

- User wants a stock thesis page or research snapshot
- Need structured commentary around a single equity

## Inputs required

- company summary
- financial metrics
- valuation data
- risk factors
- catalysts

## Rules

- Separate facts from thesis
- Include downside case
- Do not present speculative assumptions as established facts

## Output format

- business overview
- bull case
- bear case
- valuation notes
- monitoring checklist

## Edge cases

- negative earnings
- cyclical businesses
- multiple business segments
- sparse coverage

## Validation checklist

- confirm input completeness
- verify units and currency conventions
- state assumptions explicitly
- return warnings instead of hiding uncertainty
